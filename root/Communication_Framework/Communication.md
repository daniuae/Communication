# 🚀 30-Day Communication Improvement Plan

## Week 1
- Watch Skillopedia (20 mins/day)
- Record yourself speaking for 2 mins daily
- Focus on pronunciation

## Week 2
- Watch Toastmasters and Alex Lyon
- Practice introducing yourself
- Improve interview answers

## Week 3
- Watch Charisma on Command
- Improve body language
- Practice storytelling

## Week 4
- Watch TEDx Talks
- Deliver a 5-minute presentation
- Record and review your speech
