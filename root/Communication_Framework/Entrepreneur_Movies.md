# Top 10 Entrepreneur Movies Every Aspiring Founder Should Watch

These movies are not just entertaining—they teach lessons about entrepreneurship, leadership, innovation, risk-taking, sales, negotiation, perseverance, and business strategy.

---

# 1. The Social Network (2010)

🎯 Entrepreneur Lesson:
Building a startup from scratch, scaling fast, handling co-founders, and dealing with legal battles.

📖 Story:
The journey of Facebook from a college project to a billion-dollar company.

💡 Key Learnings:
- Startup execution matters more than ideas
- Importance of partnerships
- Equity distribution challenges
- Growth hacking

⭐ Entrepreneur Rating: 10/10

---

# 2. Steve Jobs (2015)

🎯 Entrepreneur Lesson:
Product vision, innovation, leadership, and perfectionism.

📖 Story:
Focuses on three major product launches in Steve Jobs' career.

💡 Key Learnings:
- Customer-centric innovation
- Product obsession
- Leadership under pressure
- Building world-class teams

⭐ Entrepreneur Rating: 10/10

---

# 3. Jobs (2013)

🎯 Entrepreneur Lesson:
Starting from a garage and building a technology empire.

📖 Story:
The rise of Steve Jobs and Apple.

💡 Key Learnings:
- Visionary thinking
- Persistence
- Product-market fit
- Branding

⭐ Entrepreneur Rating: 9/10

---

# 4. The Founder (2016)

🎯 Entrepreneur Lesson:
Franchising, business scaling, systems, and real estate strategy.

📖 Story:

💡 Key Learnings:
- Systems beat talent
- Importance of scalability
- Franchise business model
- Real estate wealth creation

⭐ Entrepreneur Rating: 10/10

---

# 5. Joy (2015)

🎯 Entrepreneur Lesson:

- Personal resilience
- Self-belief

⭐ Entrepreneur Rating: 9/10
