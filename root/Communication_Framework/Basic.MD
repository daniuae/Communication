# Daily 30-Minute Communication Improvement Plan

## Step 1 (5 Minutes)

Watch a pronunciation video.

Example Channels:

- Rachel's English
- BBC Learning English
- English with Lucy

---

## Step 2 (10 Minutes)

Practice Shadowing.

### Example

Speaker:

> "I would like to discuss the project timeline."

Pause the video.

Repeat exactly:

- Same speed
- Same tone
- Same pronunciation

Repeat 5 times.

---

## Step 3 (10 Minutes)

Record Yourself

Speak for 1 minute about:

- Your day
- Your work
- Your project
- Your learning

Listen and compare with native speakers.

Focus on:

- Pronunciation
- Speed
- Pauses
- Clarity

---

## Step 4 (5 Minutes)

Speak Freely

Topics:

- What I learned today
- My project status
- My future goals
- My weekend plans

No reading.

Speak naturally.

# Common MTI Mistakes Made by Indian Speakers

| Incorrect | Correct |
|------------|----------|
| Is it? | Really? |
| Discuss about | Discuss |
| Revert back | Reply |
| Myself Ravi | I am Ravi |
| Passed out in 2020 | Graduated in 2020 |
| Do one thing | Here's what we can do |
