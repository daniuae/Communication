# 1. Vision & Goals

A successful Communication Trainer must master:

- Communication Skills
- Public Speaking
- Facilitation Skills
- Coaching Skills
- Adult Learning Principles
- Behavioral Psychology
- Presentation Design
- Feedback Techniques

The goal is not simply to speak well.

The goal is to help others communicate effectively.

# 2. Communication Trainer Competency Model

## Level 1: Personal Communication

Learn:

- Voice Modulation
- Pronunciation
- Fluency
- Vocabulary
- Storytelling
- Listening Skills

### Daily Activities

- Read aloud for 10 minutes
- Record yourself speaking
- Shadow professional speakers
- Listen to TED Talks

---

## Level 2: Public Speaking

Learn:

- Stage Presence
- Eye Contact
- Body Language
- Audience Engagement
- Confidence Building

### Daily Activities

- 2-minute impromptu speaking
- Presentation recording
- Mirror practice
- Storytelling exercises

---

## Level 3: Facilitation

Learn:

- Conduct workshops
- Handle participants
- Ask powerful questions
- Create engagement

### Daily Activities

- Conduct mock training sessions
- Group discussions
- Facilitation simulations

---

## Level 4: Coaching

Learn:

- Observation techniques
- Feedback methods
- Behavioral change coaching
- Confidence coaching

### Daily Activities

- Observe speeches
- Evaluate speakers
- Provide structured feedback

# 3. Communication Trainer Syllabus (16 Weeks)

---

## Week 1: Foundations of Communication

### Topics

- What is communication?
- Communication process
- Barriers to communication
- Verbal vs Non-Verbal communication

### Practical

- Introduce yourself in 60 seconds
- Record and evaluate

---

## Week 2: Voice and Pronunciation

### Topics

- Pitch
- Pace
- Pause
- Volume
- Stress and Intonation

### Practical

Read aloud daily:

- Newspaper articles
- Blog posts
- TED Talk transcripts

---

## Week 3: English Fluency Development

### Topics

- Thinking in English
- Sentence structures
- Everyday communication

### Practical

Speak continuously for:

- 2 minutes
- 3 minutes
- 5 minutes

without stopping.

---

## Week 4: Body Language

### Topics

- Posture
- Gestures
- Facial expressions
- Eye contact

### Practical

Deliver a speech without slides.

Record and analyze:

- Confidence
- Gestures
- Eye movement

---

## Week 5: Storytelling

### Topics

- Business storytelling
- Emotional storytelling
- Technical storytelling

### Framework

SPARL

```text
Situation
Problem
Action
Result
Lesson
```

### Practical

Transform a technical project into a story.

---

## Week 6: Listening Skills

### Topics

- Active Listening
- Reflective Listening
- Empathetic Listening

### Practical

Watch a TED Talk and summarize:

- Main message
- Story
- Key takeaway

---

## Week 7: Presentation Skills

### Topics

- Presentation structure
- Openings
- Closings
- Slide design

### Practical

Create a 5-slide presentation.

Present for 5 minutes.

---

## Week 8: Public Speaking

### Topics

- Fear management
- Confidence
- Stage presence

### Practical

Deliver a speech to:

- Camera
- Friends
- Colleagues

---

## Week 9: Executive Communication

### Topics

- Pyramid Principle
- Stakeholder communication
- Business writing

### Practical

Present a business recommendation.

---

## Week 10: Persuasion

### Topics

Aristotle's Model:

```text
Ethos
Pathos
Logos
```

### Practical

Persuade someone to:

- Buy a product
- Join a project
- Accept an idea

---

## Week 11: Facilitation Skills

### Topics

- Conducting workshops
- Group activities
- Handling difficult participants

### Practical

Facilitate a 15-minute session.

---

## Week 12: Adult Learning Principles

### Topics

- How adults learn
- Learning styles
- Retention techniques

### Practical

Teach a topic using:

- Explain
- Demonstrate
- Practice
- Feedback

---

## Week 13: Coaching Skills

### Topics

- Coaching vs Training
- Powerful questioning
- Feedback techniques

### Practical

Conduct a coaching conversation.

---

## Week 14: Difficult Conversations

### Topics

- Conflict resolution
- Giving feedback
- Managing objections

### Practical

Role play:

- Manager
- Employee
- Customer

---

## Week 15: Assessment Design

### Topics

- Evaluation techniques
- Rubrics
- Skill assessment

### Practical

Create an assessment sheet.

---

## Week 16: Communication Trainer Certification Project

### Deliver:

- 90-minute workshop
- PPT
- Activities
- Evaluation Sheet
- Feedback Form

---

# 5. Communication Labs

---

## Lab 1: Voice Lab

Daily

```text
Read for 10 minutes

Record

Listen

Improve
```

---

## Lab 2: Pronunciation Lab

Practice:

```text
TH vs T

V vs W

R sounds

Word Stress
```

Examples:

```text
Think vs Tink

Very vs Wary

Work vs Vork
```

---

## Lab 3: Storytelling Lab

Convert:

```text
Facts → Stories
```

Example:

```text
Project saved $1 million.
```

to

```text
Situation
Problem
Action
Result
Lesson
```
